Navigating GASB 103, 104 & 105:
What Governments Need to Know

Program Description

Stay current with the latest Governmental Accounting Standards Board (GASB) updates. This training provides an overview of GASB Statements No. 103, 104, and 105, focusing on key requirements, implementation considerations, and practical impacts on governmental financial reporting.

Join us on Thursday, May 14 at 10:00 AM PT in a comprehensive webinar on GASB 103, 104 & 105 best practices. This session will guide you through key elements, common challenges, and strategies for a smooth transition.

Key Topics

  • Overview of Statements GASB 103, 104, and 105 and why they were issued
  • Key changes compared to current reporting and disclosure practices
  • Expected impacts on financial statements, notes, and ACFR preparation
  • Effective dates and what governments should be doing now to prepare
